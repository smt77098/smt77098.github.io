Making the Legs

To hop, a frog needs legs!

This is what your frog should look like if you followed all the steps from the previous page.

The next step is to start on the back legs. Take the bottom part of the frog and fold up to meet the edge of the triangle. Fold in half to make a crease, then unfold the second fold you just made, keeping the first.

Fold the left side of the paper towards the center until it reaches the middle. Repeat with right side.

Now unfold a little, but pinch the sides and fold the bottom part of the paper until it reaches the middle again. If you did it right, The bottom part of the paper should look like a upside down trapazoid.

You are almost there! Continue to the next step to finish the legs for your frog so you can start hopping around!
