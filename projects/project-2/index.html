Welcome!

Origami is the traditional Japanese art of paper folding. It involves creating intricate and beautiful shapes by folding a single sheet of paper without the use of scissors or glue.

With just a few simple folds, you can transform just a plain sheet of paper into anything you can imagine or want. If you want something easy for your first project, check out our paper frog tutorial!
