The First Folds

To create an origami frog, start by folding a square peice of paper in half, creasing along the edge.

Take the top edge of the folded paper, and fold it till it meets the left edge. The fold should look like a triangle.

Crease the fold firmly along the edge to make it sharp.

Unfold, and repeat with the opposite corner.

There should be an “X” shape out of the folds you made. Put your fingers on the left and right sides, pushing up so the paper starts to pinch. Fold it down. It should look like there is a triangle.

Once you have completed this step, your paper should be folded neatly in half, and you'll have a triangle shape at the top. This is the first step in creating your origami frog.

Continue to the next step to unfold and proceed with the folding process.
